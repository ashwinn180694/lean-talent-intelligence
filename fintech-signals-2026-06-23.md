# 📡 Lean Fintech Signals — Week of June 23, 2026

## 🔥 This week's highlights

The week's biggest signal is OKX launching a Mastercard-powered stablecoin spending card across Europe (June 20), cementing the crypto-to-card trend that's reshaping the payments landscape. Mollie doubled down on its pan-European ambitions with a €350M expansion commitment across all 30 EEA markets. Uniswap accelerated its push into mainstream fintech by opening its liquidity infrastructure to apps and AI agents — a signal that DeFi rails are quietly becoming enterprise infrastructure.

---

## Signals by company

**OKX** · Product Launch / Expansion
Launched a Mastercard-powered card on June 20 enabling European users to spend USDC or USDG stablecoins at point of sale, with real-time conversion to EUR. This follows OKX securing an EU payments institution licence in February 2026 and signals aggressive retail expansion in regulated European markets.
Source: [PYMNTS](https://www.pymnts.com/cryptocurrency/2026/okx-and-mastercard-enable-stablecoin-payments-for-european-cardholders/)

**Uniswap** · Product Launch / Expansion
On June 18, Uniswap announced an ecosystem expansion making its liquidity accessible via wallets, APIs, and AI agents — explicitly targeting fintech apps and agentic commerce. UNI token surged 23% on June 20 after Standard Chartered projected a $100 price target by 2030.
Source: [CoinMarketCap](https://coinmarketcap.com/cmc-ai/uniswap/latest-updates/)

**Mollie** · Expansion
On June 19, Mollie announced a €350M commitment to fund full coverage across all 30 EEA markets, with development hubs already live in Milan, Stockholm, and Warsaw, and Lisbon to follow. This runs concurrent with the pending acquisition of GoCardless (announced Dec 2025, closing expected mid-2026), which would create Europe's broadest combined card + bank payment platform.
Sources: [Fintech Futures](https://www.fintechfutures.com/paytech/mollie-iceland-croatia-launch-350m-growth-commitment), [Fintech Global](https://fintech.global/2026/06/19/mollie-bets-e350m-on-borderless-european-commerce/)

**Klarna** · Product Launch
Klarna launched a US mobile phone plan offering unlimited 5G talk, text, and data for $40/month via AT&T's network (powered by Gigs infrastructure). Available to its 26M+ US users, with UK and Germany to follow. This is Klarna's most significant product move post-IPO (listed NYSE as KLAR, September 2025), extending the brand from BNPL into everyday financial lifestyle.
Sources: [FinTech Weekly](https://www.fintechweekly.com/magazine/articles/klarna-launches-unlimited-mobile-plan-us-fintech-telecom-shift), [Gigs](https://gigs.com/press/klarna-expands-into-mobile-phone-plans-with-flexible-no-hassle-service-for-25m-americans)

**Pleo** · Layoffs
Around June 12, Pleo laid off approximately 50 employees in engineering, data, product, and design roles — its second significant cut in eight months (100 commercial staff were let go in November 2025). The cuts were framed around AI-driven efficiency, notably timed one day after Pleo launched a finance AI agent. Affected employees were primarily in Denmark, UK, and Germany.
Sources: [The Next Web](https://thenextweb.com/news/pleo-layoffs-ai-agents-finance), [Sifted](https://sifted.eu/articles/pleo-lays-off-50-employees)

**Nuvei** · Acquisition
In mid-June, Canadian payments processor Nuvei was reported to be in advanced negotiations to acquire US-based cross-border paytech Payoneer for approximately $2.7 billion, with a deal potentially finalisable within days. This would be Nuvei's largest acquisition and a major cross-border payments consolidation move.
Source: [Fintech Futures](https://www.fintechfutures.com/m-a/nuvei-reportedly-in-talks-to-acquire-payoneer)

**Fireblocks** · Product Launch
On June 11, Fireblocks introduced ETH Staking Link — a standardised interface connecting staking providers with Fireblocks' institutional custody platform. The company reports staking volume on its platform has more than doubled in the past six months, reflecting institutional Ethereum exposure becoming mainstream.
Source: [NewsBTC](https://www.newsbtc.com/news/fireblocks-institutional-ethereum-staking-standardized-rails/)

**Chainlink** · Product Launch / Partnership
On June 9, Chainlink's Runtime Environment was confirmed as the exclusive oracle infrastructure for FIFA World Cup 2026 prediction markets, covering all 104 matches. Chainlink also integrated 26 new platforms this period, with usage reported at record highs.
Source: [Bitget News](https://www.bitget.com/amp/news/detail/12560605468211)

**Temenos** · Acquisition
On June 8, Temenos announced the acquisition of additiv AG, a Swiss fintech that provides AI-driven orchestration and mass-affluent wealth management capabilities. Deal is 50% cash / 50% equity; expected to close Q3 2026. Temenos also ranked #1 in Core Banking for the 21st consecutive year in the IBS Intelligence Sales League Table (June 16).
Sources: [Temenos](https://www.temenos.com/press_release/temenos-acquires-additiv-to-strengthen-its-wealth-proposition-and-accelerate-ai-driven-orchestration/), [Fintech Futures](https://www.fintechfutures.com/m-a/temenos-acquires-additiv)

**Thunes** · Product Launch / Expansion
On June 3, Thunes launched real-time payment capabilities into the United States via its Direct Global Network, with native support for ACH, Same-Day ACH, and RTP rails through a tier-1 US bank. The US has historically been a gap in Thunes' cross-border network.
Source: [Thunes](https://www.thunes.com/news/thunes-unlocks-real-time-payments-to-the-us-from-the-rest-of-the-world)

**Airwallex** · Product Launch / Expansion
June 2026 release notes document new product features including the launch of Airwallex Billing. The company also confirmed Principal Membership in EPI, enabling Wero wallet acceptance for its European merchants. Separately, Airwallex is deploying $1.135B in the UK and EMEA over five years.
Source: [Airwallex](https://www.airwallex.com/global/blog/new-at-airwallex-june-edition-2026)

**Tabby** · Funding / IPO
Tabby's IPO on the Saudi Exchange (Tadawul) is widely expected imminently in Q2/Q3 2026, following a pre-IPO share sale that pushed its valuation to $4.5 billion. HSBC, JPMorgan, and Morgan Stanley are advising. The company serves 15M+ users and transacts $10B+ annualised volume.
Sources: [Finance Middle East](https://www.financemiddleeast.com/news/tabby-eyes-2026-ipo-as-valuation-climbs-to-3-3-billion/), [Finextra](https://www.finextra.com/newsarticle/46823/saudi-bnpl-startup-tabby-increases-valuation-to-45bn)

**Tamara** · Funding / IPO
Tamara is advancing toward what analysts expect will be one of the largest tech IPOs in the MENA region, backed by a $2.4B debt facility from Goldman Sachs and Citi secured in late 2025. No IPO date confirmed but preparations are active.
Source: [The Telegraph Middle East](https://thetelegraph.ae/business-economy/investment/uae-fintech-ipo-2026-tabby-tamara-listing-guide/)

**Nium** · Product Launch
Nium launched a stablecoin card issuance platform, enabling fintechs and banks to issue cards that settle in stablecoins. Also partnering with Circle to expand stablecoin and agentic payment capabilities.
Sources: [Nium](https://www.nium.com/newsroom/nium-launched-stablecoin-card-issuance-platform), [American Banker](https://www.americanbanker.com/payments/news/circle-and-nium-partner-to-boost-stablecoins-ai)

**Tether** · Product Launch
On June 1, Tether released TurboQuant, an open-source tool for compressing AI memory in long sessions. On June 3, Tether launched the world's first gold-backed neobanking Visa card in collaboration with Fasset. Both signal Tether's diversification beyond USDT issuance into AI tooling and product.
Source: [Tether](https://tether.io/news/)

**Polygon** · Product
Over 53% of global USDC transactions now flow through Polygon, per recent data — the most concrete signal yet of Polygon's dominance as the settlement layer for stablecoin commerce.

**Trustly** · Expansion
Trustly surpassed 120 million users globally (reported April 2026), reflecting accelerating Pay by Bank adoption ahead of its anticipated IPO. No new announcement this week but a relevant growth milestone.
Source: [Open Banking Expo](https://www.openbankingexpo.com/news/trustly-tops-120m-users-globally-as-pay-by-bank-adoption-increases/)
