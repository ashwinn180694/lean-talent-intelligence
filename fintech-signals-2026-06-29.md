**Ripple** · Regulatory
Received preliminary CASP licence from Luxembourg's CSSF on June 23 — just 8 days before the MiCA July 1 deadline. Combined with its existing EU EMI licence, Ripple can now offer full crypto and stablecoin payments infrastructure across all 30 EEA countries through a single integration. Full approval subject to final conditions. ([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2026/06/23/ripple-targets-eu-wins-preliminary-mica-approval-from-luxembourg-financial-regulator))

**Circle** · Expansion
Signed MOU with Nomura Holdings on June 25/26 to build a stablecoin-based FX settlement service for Japanese corporates — targeting Japan's $440B daily FX market. The service will let companies convert yen to USDC, settle cross-border payments in near real time (vs. 2–3 day traditional rails), and target launch in 2027. Circle stock rose ~7% on the news. ([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/06/25/circle-and-nomura-join-forces-to-target-a-usd750-billion-daily-foreign-exchange-market-in-japan), [Nikkei Asia](https://asia.nikkei.com/spotlight/cryptocurrencies/us-stablecoin-giant-circle-to-start-settlement-business-with-nomura))

**Binance** · Regulatory
Withdrew its MiCA licence application in Greece on June 24, citing anticipated rejection linked to its regulatory history and the "fit and proper" test for CZ. Will halt new EU sign-ups, spot orders, deposits, and Earn/staking products from July 1. Funds remain accessible. Plans to re-apply in France. Effectively exiting the EU market for at least several months. ([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2026/06/24/binance-withdraws-greek-mica-bid-but-vows-to-remain-in-europe))

**OKX** · Expansion
Actively recruiting Binance's EU user base ahead of the July 1 MiCA deadline. Running a deposit bonus campaign (up to 8%) for EU users migrating from unlicensed platforms, valid through July 13. OKX Europe CEO publicly stated ~80% of crypto exchanges won't survive MiCA — positioning OKX as one of the survivors with Malta MFSA CASP authorisation. ([The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/405777/okx-europe-chief-mica-deadline-nears))

**Kraken** · Expansion
Actively marketing to EU users displaced by Binance's exit, leveraging its Central Bank of Ireland CASP authorisation. Blog post titled "MiCA enforcement begins July 1 — switch to one of Europe's longest-standing licensed exchanges" published this week. ([Kraken Blog](https://blog.kraken.com/news/industry-news/europe-mica-switch))

**BitGo** · Layoffs

Shares fell ~5% to $4.80, now 73% below the January 2026 IPO price of $18. Company still has 51 open roles posted. ([The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/406266/bitgo-layoff-ai-infrastructure), [Unchained](https://unchainedcrypto.com/bitgo-cuts-nearly-15-of-workforce-pivoting-to-ai-infrastructure-and-stablecoins/))

([CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/06/25/uniswap-spark-aim-to-build-stablecoin-fx-market-as-banks-fintechs-enter-the-industry))

Investors pledge ADX-listed shares as collateral through the Wio app with no paperwork or branch visit. Retains dividends and market upside during the financing period. ([Zawya](https://www.zawya.com/en/press-release/companies-news/wio-bank-and-adx-enable-investors-to-borrow-cash-against-their-adx-listed-shares-xf5m8o9t))

([PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/fireblocks-trust-company-and-figment-enable-staking-under-qualified-custody-for-leading-near-digital-asset-treasury-svrn-302808753.html))

**Brex** · Product Launch
